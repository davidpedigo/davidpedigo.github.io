About Us

Professional & Comfortable

We strive for the best in professionalism and customer service bringing you the brightest colors, custom designs and cleanest shop in Madison, Indiana. Registered with the state of Indiana, Board of Health inspected & approved. Everyone at Paramount Art & Tattoo Gallery has been certified for completing training on blood-borne pathogens.

Clean & Beautiful

For the tattoo procedure, all equipment is single use (disposable). Sterile pre-packaged needle & tube, ink, ink caps, ointment, rubber bands, dental bib, chair & equipment covers and razor are used for each client. After each tattoo the station is broke down, and our work area and chair are properly sanitized to meet OSHA (Occupational Safety & Health Administration) standards. All of our work at Paramount Art & Tattoo Gallery is guaranteed (Free touch ups if needed with in 6 months). Come by the shop today, ask questions, buy some beautiful original art, and see what Paramount Art & Tattoo Gallery has to offer.
